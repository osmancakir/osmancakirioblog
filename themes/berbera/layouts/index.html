Data Science & Creative Coding Notes
Because why not?

I am a data scientist formally trained as a quantitative economist. Currently, I am doing a Ph.D in Art History at Ludwig-Maximilians University of Munich and working with art metadata to explore what digitalization could bring to Art History literature and I am very much excited about it.

I would like to help dissipate clouds around the fuzzy terms 'Artificial Intelligence' and 'Machine Learning'. In this regard, I want to write small applications for possible use cases of ML/AI to show you how useful it is for humanity. I hope my work here would be a small contribution to eradicate the meaningless fear and perplexity around the topic.

I am also happy that now I have a place to share my interests and thoughts.
